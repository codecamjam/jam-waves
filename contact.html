Thanks for visiting my site! I'm a solo musician based in sunny Jacksonville, FL. I provide a live one man band show that's unlike any solo act you've ever seen! Basically, my show is a nonstop, 100% live-looped, instrumental jam session. You'll see me play guitar, bass, keys, bongos, drum machines, gizmos, and so much more! And it's all me, live-looped, and made in real time! I may also have some ambient nature sounds playing in the background from time to time, so it's a very relaxing, refreshing kind of vibe.

I provide an entertaining and musically engaging show. At the same time, TFT is more of an ambiance than it is a traditional musical performance. All I want to do is jam out at a comfortable audio level while you and your friends can still have conversations with one another without yelling over the music. So get ready to be jammed, kick back, and watch me play with my Toys From Taiwan!
